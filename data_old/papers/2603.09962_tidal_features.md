# 2603.09962 - Tidal Features in Galaxy Pairs: A Deep Learning Approach

**Status:** analyzed  
**Relevance:** high  
**Concepts:** tidal-features, galaxy-pairs, morphological-transformation, deep-learning

## Summary
Deep learning (TidalNet) achieves 94% accuracy detecting tidal features in 2,847 galaxy pairs. 23% show tidal features, with detection rate increasing with closer separations and more equal mass ratios.

## Key Quantities
- Detection accuracy: 94%
- Tidal feature fraction: 23%
- Sample size: 2,847 galaxy pairs
- Features: tidal tails, bridges, shells, asymmetric structures

## Galaxy Evolution Relevance
Tidal interactions are common (23% of pairs) and drive morphological transformation. Deep learning enables systematic studies at scale.
