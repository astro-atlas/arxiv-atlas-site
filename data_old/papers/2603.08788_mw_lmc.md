# 2603.08788 - Milky Way-LMC Interaction from Halo Kinematics

**Status:** analyzed  
**Relevance:** medium  
**Concepts:** milky-way-mass, lmc-mass, reflex-motion, dynamical-modeling, satellite-interactions, velocity-anisotropy

## Summary
H3+SEGUE+MagE survey of outer halo stars (to 160 kpc) constrains MW and LMC masses via reflex motion from LMC pericentric passage. MW mass M(<50 kpc) = 3.63 ± 0.16 × 10^11 M☉, LMC mass = 9.74^{+2.07}_{-1.81} × 10^10 M☉. LMC/MW mass ratio at least ~20%.

## Key Quantities
- Reflex velocity: 39.4^{+7.6}_{-7.2} km/s
- MW mass M(<50 kpc): 3.63 ± 0.16 × 10^11 M☉
- LMC mass M(<50 kpc): 9.74^{+2.07}_{-1.81} × 10^10 M☉
- LMC/MW ratio: at least ~20%
- Velocity anisotropy: β_0 = 0.61 ± 0.03

## Galaxy Evolution Relevance
MW-LMC interaction provides local laboratory for satellite galaxy dynamics. Reflex motion constrains both galaxy masses.
