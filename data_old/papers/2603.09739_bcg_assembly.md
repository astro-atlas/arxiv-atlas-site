# 2603.09739 - Stellar Mass Assembly of Brightest Cluster Galaxies

**Status:** analyzed  
**Relevance:** high  
**Concepts:** brightest-cluster-galaxies, mass-assembly, dry-mergers, galaxy-growth, cluster-evolution

## Summary
Spitzer/IRAC survey of 450 BCGs shows they grow by factor of ~4 since z~1. Growth dominated by dry mergers at z<1, star formation at z>1. Most massive BCGs show accelerated growth.

## Key Quantities
- Sample: 450 BCGs
- Mass growth: factor of ~4 since z~1
- z<1: dry mergers dominate
- z>1: star formation contributes
- Most massive: accelerated growth, enhanced merger rates

## Galaxy Evolution Relevance
BCGs are most massive galaxies. Their assembly history constrains extreme end of galaxy formation and role of mergers.
