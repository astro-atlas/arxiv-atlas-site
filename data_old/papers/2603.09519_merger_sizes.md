# 2603.09519 - Merger Impact on Galaxy Sizes and Structure

**Status:** analyzed  
**Relevance:** high  
**Concepts:** galaxy-mergers, size-evolution, sersic-index, structural-parameters, candels-survey

## Summary
CANDELS study of 1,200 merging systems shows major mergers increase sizes by 30-50% and Sérsic indices by Δn ≈ 0.5-1.0. Gas-rich mergers show more pronounced changes. Relaxation time ~1-2 Gyr.

## Key Quantities
- Sample: 1,200 merging systems
- Size increase: 30-50% from major mergers
- Sérsic index increase: Δn ≈ 0.5-1.0
- Gas-rich mergers: more pronounced effects
- Relaxation time: ~1-2 Gyr

## Galaxy Evolution Relevance
Mergers quantifiably modify galaxy structure. Gas content affects merger outcomes. Post-merger relaxation is measurable.
