# 2603.08830 - Stellar-to-Halo Mass Relation from Weak Lensing

**Status:** analyzed  
**Relevance:** high  
**Concepts:** stellar-to-halo-mass-relation, weak-lensing, dark-matter-halos, star-formation-efficiency, feedback

## Summary
DES and HSC weak lensing constraints show peak stellar mass fraction at M_halo ~ 10^12 M☉, with lower fractions at higher and lower masses. Weak redshift evolution with peak shifting to higher masses at higher z.

## Key Quantities
- Surveys: DES and HSC weak lensing
- Peak stellar fraction: M_halo ~ 10^12 M☉
- Lower fractions: M_halo < 10^11.5 and > 10^12.5 M☉
- Redshift evolution: weak, peak shifts to higher M_halo at higher z
- Regulation: feedback + gravity

## Galaxy Evolution Relevance
SHMR connects galaxies to dark matter halos. Shape and evolution constrain feedback processes and SF regulation.
