# 2603.09905 - AGN Feedback in Galaxy Clusters: Abell 119

**Status:** analyzed  
**Relevance:** high  
**Concepts:** agn-feedback, x-ray-cavities, cluster-cooling, bcg-evolution, mechanical-energy-injection

## Summary
Multi-wavelength study of Abell 119 reveals three generations of X-ray cavities (10-100 Myr), episodic AGN activity with ~10% duty cycle, and total mechanical energy injection of ~10^61 erg.

## Key Quantities
- Cavity generations: 3 (ages 10-100 Myr)
- AGN duty cycle: ~10%
- Total mechanical energy: ~10^61 erg
- Metallicity enhancements in cavity wakes

## Galaxy Evolution Relevance
AGN feedback offsets cooling in cluster cores and transports enriched material. Episodic activity maintains long-term heating.
