# 2603.09879 - MUSE Deep Survey: Star Formation Histories at z~1

**Status:** analyzed  
**Relevance:** high  
**Concepts:** star-formation-history, downsizing, muse-survey, spectral-fitting, mass-assembly

## Summary
Bayesian spectral fitting of 412 MUSE galaxies at z~1 shows massive galaxies (M*>10^10.5 M☉) formed 70% of their mass by z~2, while low-mass galaxies continued forming stars later. SFH diversity increases with decreasing mass.

## Key Quantities
- Sample: 412 galaxies at z~1
- Massive galaxy formation: 70% by z~2
- Lookback time coverage: 0-10 Gyr
- SFH modes: bursty and continuous in low-mass galaxies

## Galaxy Evolution Relevance
Direct evidence for downsizing: massive galaxies form earlier. Low-mass galaxies show diverse formation pathways.
