# 2603.09559 - Galaxy Spin and Morphology Connection

**Status:** analyzed  
**Relevance:** high  
**Concepts:** spin-parameter, galaxy-morphology, angular-momentum, dynamical-state, morphological-bimodality

## Summary
GALEX-SDSS-WISE study of 5,000 galaxies shows bimodality in spin-morphology space: early-types at λ < 0.5, late-types at λ ≈ 0.6. Transition at λ ≈ 0.5. Spin is fundamental parameter governing galaxy structure.

## Key Quantities
- Sample: 5,000 galaxies
- Early-type spin: λ < 0.5
- Late-type spin: λ ≈ 0.6
- Transition: λ ≈ 0.5
- Spin parameter: λ = J|E|^(1/2)/GM^(5/2)

## Galaxy Evolution Relevance
Spin-morphology connection links cosmological initial conditions to observed structures. Angular momentum governs formation pathways.
