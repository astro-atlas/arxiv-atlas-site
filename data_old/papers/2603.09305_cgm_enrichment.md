# 2603.09305 - Chemical Enrichment of the Circumgalactic Medium

**Status:** analyzed  
**Relevance:** high  
**Concepts:** circumgalactic-medium, chemical-enrichment, outflows, gas-accretion, quasar-absorption

## Summary
500 quasar sightlines probing CGM at z~0.1-0.5 show metallicity increases with galaxy mass as Z_CGM ∝ M*^0.3, reaching ~0.3 Z☉ for M* ~ 10^11 M☉. Gradient extends to ~100 kpc.

## Key Quantities
- Sample: 500 quasar sightlines
- Metallicity-mass relation: Z_CGM ∝ M*^0.3
- Maximum metallicity: ~0.3 Z☉ at M* ~ 10^11 M☉
- Gradient extent: ~100 kpc
- Mechanism: metal-rich outflows + pristine accretion

## Galaxy Evolution Relevance
CGM is critical interface between galaxies and IGM. Chemical enrichment constrains feedback, gas cycling, and metal transport.
