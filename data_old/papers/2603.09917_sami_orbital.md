# 2603.09917 - SAMI Survey: Internal Orbital Structure Across the Color-Magnitude Diagram

**Status:** analyzed  
**Relevance:** high  
**Concepts:** galaxy-bimodality, orbital-anisotropy, dynamical-modelling, jeans-modelling, quenching

## Summary
JAM dynamical modeling of 2,945 SAMI galaxies shows red sequence galaxies have more radially biased orbits (β ≈ 0.3) than blue cloud galaxies (β ≈ 0.1). Transition from rotation to pressure support at M* ≈ 10^10.5 M☉.

## Key Quantities
- Sample: 2,945 galaxies
- Red sequence anisotropy: β ≈ 0.3
- Blue cloud anisotropy: β ≈ 0.1
- Transition mass: M* ≈ 10^10.5 M☉

## Galaxy Evolution Relevance
Direct dynamical evidence for galaxy bimodality. Orbital properties differ between star-forming and quiescent galaxies, with transition at established quenching mass scale.
