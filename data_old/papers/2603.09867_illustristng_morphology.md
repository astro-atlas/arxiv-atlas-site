# 2603.09867 - Morphological Transformation in IllustrisTNG

**Status:** analyzed  
**Relevance:** high  
**Concepts:** morphological-transformation, galaxy-mergers, illustristng, agn-feedback, secular-evolution, simulation

## Summary
IllustrisTNG tracking of 10,000 galaxies shows major mergers (mass ratio >0.25) form 60% of ellipticals, minor mergers 25%, secular processes 15%. Morphological mix evolves from 80% disks at z~2 to 50% at z~0.

## Key Quantities
- Sample: 10,000 galaxies tracked z=4 to z=0
- Major merger contribution: 60%
- Minor merger contribution: 25%
- Secular contribution: 15%
- Disk fraction: 80% at z~2 → 50% at z~0

## Galaxy Evolution Relevance
Mergers dominate morphological transformation but secular processes contribute significantly. AGN feedback maintains quiescence in massive ellipticals.
